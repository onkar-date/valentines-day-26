Our Journey

Welcome to our story...

From the very first "Hey" to this moment,
every day has been a chapter in our beautiful journey.

This is our timeline of love, laughter, and memories -
from Day One to today.

Swipe through our moments together

The Beginning

"Hey"

Just one word.
Three letters that started everything.

Sometimes the most important conversations
begin with the simplest words.

April 13

It started with rain.

Two hours that felt like a lifetime in a moment.

You reached home soaked, but my heart felt full.
I donated blood that day, maybe a sign of giving a piece of myself away.

The Everyday Phase

You always called me Makad
"Divasbhar nusta lolat astos!" you’d tease, and I’d pretend to protest.

But those silly, cozy days - just us, talking about everything and nothing - became my favorite memories.

Who knew a Makad could feel so loved?

July 1

I said goodbye.

My heart broke with every word, but I thought it was for the best.
Sometimes we hurt the people we love when we're lost ourselves.
I was drowning in my own confusion.

Letting you go was the hardest thing I'd ever done.

September 5

"Hey Onkar kasa ahes."

A simple message, but it felt like sunlight after a long rain.
Sometimes, the smallest words can restart a heartbeat.
You found your way back to me.

Dubai

Different place, same connection.
Miles apart, but you were always close in my thoughts.

Distance only made me realize how much you mean to me.
Sometimes, being apart can make the heart grow fonder.

September 13

We met again at Si Nonnas.

After everything, seeing you across the table felt like coming home.
The world faded, and it was just us.

Some places become sacred because of who you share them with.

November 10

My birthday. Same place.

Celebrating with you made the day unforgettable.
You made me feel seen, cherished, loved.

It wasn’t about the party, it was about us.

Pizza Day

We made pizza together.

It was messy, imperfect, and so much fun.
We laughed, we learned.

I love that we can make anything work, as long as we’re together.

November 28

Celebrating you felt like celebrating us.

I admire your ambition, your strength, your heart.
You inspire me to be better.

I’m so proud of you.

December 27

Watching you light up at something you always wanted to see.

Your happiness is my happiness.
I love seeing you smile, truly smile.
Those moments are everything to me.

January 4

Our families met.

Something shifted, suddenly, it all felt real.
No rush, no pressure.
Just a quiet certainty that this is right.

Small Moments

Bowling, dinners, silly conversations.

It’s the little things that built our love.
The laughter, the comfort, the feeling of belonging.
Maybe the small moments are the ones that matter most.

Now

Lately, things have felt heavy.

We’ve stumbled, but we haven’t let go.
I believe in us, in our ability to weather any storm.
Love isn’t always easy, but it’s always worth it.

Shital,

I still smile when I think about the disciplined gym girl I met on April 13th: strong, focused, and quietly ambitious.

I truly admire your consistency and the way you never give up. It inspires me more than you know.

Sometimes I look for a deeper emotional connection, and in that process I may say or do things impulsively.
But you know where my heart is.

I don't want to rush you.
I don't want to pressure you.
I just want to build something steady and real, with patience.

Maybe we're still writing our story, one chapter at a time.
And if there's one thing I'm certain about...

It's that I'm here.
Always.
